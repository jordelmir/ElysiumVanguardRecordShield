RECORD SHIELD

Privacy Policy — Last updated: July 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Record Shield ("we", "our", "the app") is designed to protect individuals by providing secure, tamper-resistant audio and video evidence recording. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

We do NOT collect personal information. The app operates entirely on your device.

2.1 Data Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

  • Audio/Video recordings: Stored exclusively in the app's private internal storage. They are invisible to other apps, file managers, and gallery applications.
  • PIN hash: A cryptographically hashed version of your 6-digit PIN (PBKDF2 + AndroidKeyStore). Your actual PIN is never stored.
  • Device registration token: If you choose to use cloud backup, a device-specific API token is stored encrypted.

2.2 Data Sent to Cloud (Only If You Configure It)

  • Evidence chunks: 10-second audio/video fragments are uploaded only to the cloud provider YOU select (Google Drive or Supabase). We do not operate any servers that store your recordings.
  • Device fingerprint: A hashed identifier used to associate uploads with your device. This is a one-way hash and cannot be reversed.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Local recording: Audio and video are captured and stored only on your device.
  • Cloud backup (optional): If you enable cloud storage, encrypted fragments are uploaded to your chosen provider. This is entirely optional and user-controlled.
  • We do NOT: Sell, share, analyze, profile, or monetize your data in any way.
  • We do NOT: Use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do NOT: Access your recordings unless you explicitly share them.

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • CAMERA: Required for video evidence capture. Used only when you start a recording.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: Required for audio evidence capture. Used only when you start a recording.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required by Android to keep recording active when the screen is off.
  • INTERNET: Required only if you configure cloud backup.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required by Android 13+ for the foreground service notification.

We do NOT request: location, contacts, files, SMS, phone state, or any other sensitive permissions.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption at rest: All sensitive data (PINs, tokens) are encrypted using Android Keystore (hardware-backed AES-256-GCM).
  • Encryption in transit: All cloud communication uses TLS 1.3 (HTTPS).
  • Integrity: Every evidence chunk is SHA-256 hashed. Any tampering is detectable.
  • Isolation: Recordings are stored in app-private internal storage, invisible to other apps.
  • No backups: Data extraction rules explicitly exclude all app data from device backups.

6. Cloud Storage Providers

If you configure cloud backup:

  • Google Drive: Your recordings are stored in YOUR Google Drive account under a "RecordShield" folder. We do not have access to this data. Google's own privacy policy applies to data stored on their servers.
  • Supabase: If you use the Supabase backend, your data is stored in a private bucket with Row Level Security. Only your device can access its own data.

7. Data Sharing

You can manually share recordings with others via the app's share function. This is entirely your choice. We do not share your data with any third parties.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: privacy@recordshield.app

11. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process data based on:

  • Consent: You explicitly grant camera, microphone, and notification permissions.
  • Legitimate interest: The app's core purpose is personal safety and evidence preservation.

You have the right to: access, delete, and export your data at any time through the app.
